*River Mist Chronicles* doesn’t aim for shock or spectacle. It prefers small, careful observations and melodies that move at a walking pace. The production keeps the edges rough enough to feel lived in, which makes the quiet songs land harder.

The record’s strength is consistency. Nothing spikes, but nothing falls flat either. The mid-album stretch is particularly strong, leaning into harmony stacks and brushed percussion that keep the mood warm without becoming sentimental.

This show had the looseness of a band that trusts its songs. The Monarch is a tight room, but Atlas Park treated it like a wide stage, stretching arrangements and letting the crowd do some of the work.

The encore was the night’s best moment: no effects, no rush, just the band and a room willing to sing quietly together. It’s the kind of set that feels less like a performance and more like a shared memory.
